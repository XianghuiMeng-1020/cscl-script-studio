## 技术细节

### Initial Idea 字段信息

```html
<textarea id="specInitialIdea" rows="4" 
  placeholder="e.g. I want students to compare two examples in groups and agree on which one works better...">
</textarea>
```

**访问方式**:
```python
initial_idea = driver.find_element(By.ID, "specInitialIdea")
initial_idea.send_keys("I want a simple comparison activity")
```
